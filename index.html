Case Study: Autonomous Solar Marine Platform

Project Goal

The client needed a lightweight, durable solar-integrated hull for an autonomous marine vehicle capable of long-duration operation in harsh environments.

Our Solution

We designed and fabricated composite and fiberglass panels integrated with high-efficiency solar cells. Our modular approach allowed rapid prototyping and easy maintenance.

Results

  • Increased solar energy capture by 35% compared to previous designs
  • Lightweight structure reduced overall weight by 20%
  • Completed fabrication and integration in under 6 weeks
