About Our Company

We specialize in the development and fabrication of lightweight fiberglass solar panels for demanding applications including marine systems, autonomous vehicles, and off-grid energy platforms.

Our manufacturing approach focuses on durability, weight reduction, and custom solar module construction for projects that require specialized solutions beyond standard commercial panels.

We work closely with engineers, startups, and renewable energy projects to develop solar modules suited for real-world environmental conditions.
