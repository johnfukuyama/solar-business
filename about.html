About Our Company

We specialize in manufacturing fiberglass reinforced solar panels for demanding environments including marine applications, autonomous systems, and off-grid energy projects.

Our workshop focuses on custom fabrication techniques that allow us to produce lightweight, durable solar modules designed for real-world performance.

We work with engineers, startups, and renewable energy projects that require specialized solar solutions.
