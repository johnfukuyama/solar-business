Selected Projects

Examples of custom fiberglass solar panels built for marine, research, and off-grid applications.

Autonomous Marine Vehicle Panels

Lightweight fiberglass solar modules built for long-duration marine research vehicles. Panels were designed to withstand saltwater exposure, vibration, and continuous outdoor use.

Research Vessel Solar System

Custom panel sizes were produced to match the deck geometry of a 24-foot autonomous research vessel. The modules provide reliable renewable power for onboard sensors and navigation systems.

Small-Batch Engineering Production

Panels fabricated in small production runs for engineering teams and prototype systems. Manufacturing emphasizes durability, consistent lamination, and lightweight construction.

Off-Grid Energy Systems

Custom solar modules designed for cabins, experimental platforms, and remote power systems where standard panels do not fit the available space.
